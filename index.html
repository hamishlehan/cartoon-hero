Batman

Biography

Philanthropist, businessman, and party-guy Bruce Wayne has a secret. By night, he is a superhero on a crusade to bring justice and peace to Gotham City.

A superhero in name only, Batman's actual superpowers are his vast resources and his own determination. As a young man, he overcame his crippling fear of bats by embracing it as part of his character and turning that sense of dread on to his criminal foes.
